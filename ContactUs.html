  • The Pitt Challenge The Pitt Challenge is a virtual hackathon sponsored by the University of Pittsburgh School of Pharmacy and the Clinical and Translational Science Institute (CTSI). Available to students from all walks of life, the hackathon invites everyone to engage in critical conversations regarding healthcare and pharmacy with industry giants and leading researchers.
  • Topics available for the 2021 challenge are: Virtual Experiential Learning
    • Burnout in Healthcare Workers
    • Remote Patient Interaction
    • Mobile Health Records
    • Virtual Medical Simulation
    • Intelligent Personal Assistants
    • Improving Health Literacy
    • Point of Care Testing
    • Big Data/Machine Learning in Healthcare
    • Mental Health During Social Isolation
